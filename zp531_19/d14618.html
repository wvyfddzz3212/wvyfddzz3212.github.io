Product Details

A drop-in replacement buttstock for AR15/M4 Mil-Spec carbine buffer tubes, the SL-K is designed for compact PDW style weapons or users desiring a small and light stock.

Features a shorter and slimmer profile, a smaller rubber butt-pad with rolled toe for easy shouldering and sure grip, and a 1.25" footman"s loop with the ability to accept an optional M-LOK QD Sling Mount for push-button style QD sling swivels. A new, unique, ambidextrous release latch minimizes rattle on the receiver extension without requiring additional locks, levers, or springs*.

Made in the USA.
