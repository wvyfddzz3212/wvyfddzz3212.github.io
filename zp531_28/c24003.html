説明: Features:

The COMeap 18 Pin + 10 Pin to 24 Pin ATX power cable is to convert 18 Pin + 10 Pin ports into standard 24 pin ATX port. It offers a cost-effective solution for Corsair PSUs to power the 24 pin port motherboards.

Specification:

Length(including connectors): 24.8-inch(63cm)

Connectors: 1 x 18 pin port, 1x 10 pin port , 1 x 24 pin port
