EVO Gimbals PROX2 Camera Cage for Insta360 ONE X2 

The PROX2 protects your Insta360 ONE X2 360 camera from bumps and scratches. While installed, the lightweight aluminum cage gives you multiple mounting options and access to all the camera"s buttons, ports, and controls.

The chassis of the cage features three cold shoe mounts for attaching available accessories without stressing your camera"s body. The bottom of the cage features dual mounting options - a 1/4”-20 thread for mounting your ONE X2 to monopods, tripods & selfie sticks and a folding two-prong mount that is compatible with the GoPro Ecosystem and most universal action camera mounts.
