【本の内容】
IBM SPSS Statistics 26 Step by Step: A Simple Guide and Reference
16th
edition takes a straightforward step-by-step approach that makes SPSS software clear to beginners and experienced researchers alike. Extensive use of four-color screen shots clear writing and step-by-step boxes guide readers through the program. Output for each procedure is explained and illustrated and every output term is defined. Exercises at the end of each chapter support students by providing additional opportunities to practice using SPSS.
This book covers the basics of statistical analysis and addresses more advanced topics such as multi-dimensional scaling factor analysis discriminant analysis measures of internal consistency MANOVA (between- and within-subjects) cluster analysis Log-linear models logistic regression and a chapter describing residuals. Back matter includes a description of data files used in exercises an exhaustive glossary suggestions for further reading and a comprehensive index.
