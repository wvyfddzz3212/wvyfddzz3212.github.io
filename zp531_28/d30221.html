Story
Botanician Freeman Rowel (Bruce Darn) has spent three years in the spaceship Valley Forge. It was a giant dome created within the spaceship, with a mission to cultivate the only plant specimen left on Earth. But because each plant was commanded to destroy and return the dome so Rowwell has uplift the organization to hijack the ship!! Kills your fellow astronaut while being injured yourself.
