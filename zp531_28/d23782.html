    gofanco USB 3.0 to HDMI/DVI Video Adapter is designed to add an HDMI or DVI video graphics output to your USB 3.0 enabled Ultrabook, Microsoft Surface, Apple Macbook, laptop or desktop. It supports 1080p and up to 2560x1440@50Hz over HDMI and 2040x1152@60Hz when used with the included HDMI-to-DVI adapter. It supports HD resolutions and multiple display modes including primary, extended and mirrored. gofanco USB 3.0 to HDMI/DVI Video Adapter leverages SuperSpeed USB 3.0 bandwidth of 5.0 Gbps to deliver the best performance. The included HDMI-to-DVI adapter allows you to use our adapter with DVI monitors and is convenient to bring on the road for business travelers.
