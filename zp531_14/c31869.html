説明:

This 3-in-1 set is designed for photographic studio and on-location work to trigger various flashlights. It can be suitable for most brands of camera lights, studio lights, and outdoor shooting lights by connecting the 3.5mm sync cable and 6.35mm covert adapter.

The transmitter is attached to the hot shoe of the camera while the receiver is plugged into the studio flash. Each receiver works with one studio flash.

It is compatible with most cameras such as Canon Nikon Pentax DSLR Cameras.

It has 16 channels and the transmit frequency of 433MHz can secure fast response and stable transmission.

It is equipped with a 12V 23A battery for the transmitter and two AAA batteries (not included) for the receiver.

With an umbrella input holder, the trigger can connect with a soft umbrella (flash and soft umbrella not included).
