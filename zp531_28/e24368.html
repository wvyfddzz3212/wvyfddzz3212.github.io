説明: CASEMATIX Wireless Lavalier Microphone Case Compatible with KIMAFUN 2.4g Wireless Lapel Microphone and Accessories
This compact case is designed to securely store wireless lavalier microphones and transmitter, microphone receiver, audio conversion head, windscreens, collar clip, belt clip, USB charging cable and mobile phone shunt. Safely Travel with Your Wireless Mic and Keep Accessories Organized
The compact design makes this case ideal for traveling with your wireless lavalier microphone.
