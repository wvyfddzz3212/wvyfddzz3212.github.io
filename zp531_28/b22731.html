Warm Tips:

1.The external DVD/CD drive can not work with TV/Car/Smartphone /Chromebook/Blu-ray Discs;
2.Before using it, please make sure your computer has a media player. If your device uses Windows 10/11 operating system, it is recommended to install "VLC MEDIA Player" to play DVD movies;
3.Do not put the DVD/CD drive near a strong magnetic object;
4.Please ensure your disc is firmly seated in the tray, and keep your DVD optical drive burner flat when it works;
5.The maximum read capacity is 8.5G, so the portable CD/DVD burner cannot play discs over 8.5GB;
6.When using this CD player, please connect the external power of your laptop;
7.Due to the front USB port may be unstable, suggest connecting the DVD drive to the USB port behind the computer, when you use the desktop.
