Adventures in the Pokemon Shining Pearl game will take place in the familiar Sinnoh region. Rich in nature and with mighty Mount Coronet at its heart Sinnoh is a land of many myths passed down through the ages. You’ll choose either Turtwig Chimchar or Piplup to be your first partner Pokemon and then set off on your journey to become the Champion of the Pokemon League. Along the way you’ll run into the mysterious organization Team Galactic and be able to encounter the Legendary Pokemon Palkia. Explore the revamped Grand Underground or put on a Super Contest Show The Underground from the Pokemon Pearl Version game has been powered up and is now called the Grand Underground. Here you can dig up valuable treasure and Pokemon Fossils create your own Secret Base and more! When you aren’t exploring in the Grand Underground enter a Super Contest Show! Super Contest Shows are events put on by four performers and their partner Pokemon who work together to dance and show off their skills. You can also adventure in the Grand Underground or put on a Super Contest Show with other players through the local* or online** communication of your Nintendo Switch system!
