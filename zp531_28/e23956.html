    How to use
    1:Simply plug the usb receiver into your devices(Compact keyboard mouse share a usb receiver,which is stored inside mouse for safekeeping (not stored in the keyboard);
    2: Put 2 AAA batteries into the keyboard and 1 AA battery into the mouse;
    3: Flip the switch on the mouse to the "On" position

    How to re-paring if the the mouse keyboard combo can not work
    -Keyboard Paring
    1.Put batteries into keyboard, press and hold “ESC” plus “+”, indicator starts flashing and enter into paring mode
    2.Plug USB receiver into your device. Indicator stays on for 3 seconds and pairing completed
    -Mouse Paring
    1.Put battery into mouse, switch power button on, press and hold Left, Middle, Right buttons for 5 seconds
    2.Plug USB receiver into your device. Paring is completed
