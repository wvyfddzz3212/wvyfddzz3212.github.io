Description:

With a reversible mounting plate design, a 1/4" screw and a cold shoe mount on each side, this ball head allows you to mount live monitors, LED video lights, microphones and other accessories to your DSLRs, mirrorless cameras and camcorders.

360° rotatable and 135° tiltable, the ball head allows you to position your device at an ideal angle with ease. Secure the ball head in place with the locking knob.

A combination of a rubber-padded mounting plate and a standard cold shoe bracket, the reversible plate lets you mount a variety of gadgets.

The ball head to a light stand or a small desk tripod through the 1/4” threaded hole.

Constructed with rugged aluminum alloy, this ball head can withstand a load of up to 3.3lb (1.5kg) and is lightweight for on-the-go creation.
