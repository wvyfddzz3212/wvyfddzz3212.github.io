【本の内容】
Focusing on the idea that personalizing learning starts with the learner Bray a teacher writer educational technologist professional developer coach instructional designer and creative learning strategist and McClaskey a teacher administrator professional developer leader educational technologist and consultant describe strategies and tools for teachers and learners to understand how they prefer to learn based on universal design for learning principles and learners" interests aspirations and talents. They do not address how to personalize learning but instead explain its definition including differences from differentiation and individualization; learners and how they learn best including neuroscience research on learning; changing teacher and learner roles through each stage of the process; how personalized learning is happening around the world; the rationale behind it; and the resulting shift that occurs in the school and classroom culture.
